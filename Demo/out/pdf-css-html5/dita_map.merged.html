How to use the calculator app on the iPhone

Ensure that the Calculator app is installed in phone.

  • Add numbers
  • Subtract numbers
  • Multiply numbers
  • Divide numbers
  1. Unlock your iPhone.
  2. Open the Calculator app.
  3. Enter the first number.
  4. Tap operator + for adding, - for subtraction, * for multiplication, or / for division.

  5. Enter the another number.

    Note: Tap C button to delete the entered value.

  6. Tap the = button.
